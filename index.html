Who We Are

Australia's Trusted Partner in Pipe Working Technology

Rothenberger Australia Pty Ltd is the authorised distributor of Rothenberger industrial tools across Australia, headquartered in Castle Hill, NSW. We bring over six decades of German engineering excellence to Australian industries.

From remote mining sites in Western Australia to major infrastructure projects along the eastern seaboard, our tools are trusted by contractors, engineers and project managers who demand reliability when it matters most. Our team of certified specialists provides end-to-end support — from product selection and rental planning to on-site training and servicing.
